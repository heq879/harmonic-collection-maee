There is

something on

the wall...

The collapse echoes like-

falling of mother's statue,

and the fading whisper of her lullaby.

Morphing of her gentle song-

from black to purple to a sultry azure hue,

reminiscent of cherry's maturing in rewind.

From ripe fruit to infant core,

no one ever caressed her back,

no one ever cradled her neck.

A tangled touch she long to savored,

the gasp she couldn't release in infancy's hold,

lingering in the delayed bitterness on her baby tooth.
